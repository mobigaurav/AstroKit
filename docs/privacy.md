# Privacy Policy — AstroKit

_Last updated: 28 Dec 2025_

AstroKit respects your privacy. This application is designed to be **offline-first** and does **not collect or transmit personal data to external servers**.

---

## 1. Information We Store

AstroKit stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- User-created profiles (name + date of birth)
- Birth details entered for Kundli preview (date, time, place)
- Generated astrology insights (zodiac, numerology, compatibility, kundli preview)
- App preferences (selected profile, UI state)

This data **never leaves your device**.

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

AstroKit does **not**:

- Require account creation
- Collect email addresses or phone numbers
- Track users across apps or websites
- Use analytics, ads, or third-party trackers
- Upload personal or birth data to any server

---

## 3. Offline Processing

All calculations in AstroKit are performed **on-device**, including:

- Zodiac (Sun sign) calculation
- Numerology (Life Path, Personal Year)
- Compatibility scoring
- Kundli preview generation (Phase-2)

---

## 4. Future Features (Optional & Explicit)

Future premium features may include:
- AI-generated astrology explanations
- PDF reports or exports

If enabled, these features will:
- Require **explicit user consent**
- Clearly explain what data is used
- Never store personal data without permission

This privacy policy will be updated before any such feature is released.

---

## 5. Data Control

You are always in control of your data.

From within the app, you can:
- Delete individual profiles
- Clear all saved birth details
- Reset saved insights

Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

AstroKit is **not intended for children under 13**.  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
